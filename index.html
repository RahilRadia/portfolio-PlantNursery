Built to Last

Our application is designed with reliability and ease of use in mind, ensuring that all of your business operations run smoothly and efficiently. Outlined below are the ACID properties which ensure that all transactions are valid and data integrity is maintained across the system.

Atomicity

The back-end MySQL database ensures the indivisibility of transactions by implementing fail-safe rollbacks in case of errors, ensuring that all operations are completed in their entirety or not at all.

Consistency

Enforcing all schema-defined rules and contrains maintains consistent storage and retrieval of data, making sure permanently stored information remains accurate across the board.

Isolation

By ensuring all transactions occur independently from one another, multiple users simultaneously interacting with the application experience no interference, all while data validity is preserved.

Durability

Committing transactions to storage as they occurs guarantees that all changes made in the data remain recoverable and protected from loss, even in the event of system failures.
